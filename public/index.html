About Zilter

With the global increase in the higher education enrollment by 50 million/year to an active base of 200 million students already, universities are constantly looking for quality students who are really the best fit for their institution and programs with regards to their academic grades, personality, and values. Currently, it is a manual and time-consuming process. Zilter is a unique SaaS platform for education institutes to automatically filter the quality student applications by assessing and standardizing the applications to get the best-fit match for their institute based on their own customizable requirements. It automates the whole admissions process along with increased efficiency and accuracy.

Introducing Z-Score

Designed for HEI (Higher Education Institutes ) to automate student application assessment. Zilter gives a unique score ‘Z score’ to each student profile based on qualitative and quantitative factors of the student. Which then be further refined by the university’s customizable entry requirements on a program or even individual course level. It can also predict the success probability of the student profile on the basis of the Z score of the previous successful graduates. The algorithm considers over 100 different factors and data points to find the best-fit student.
